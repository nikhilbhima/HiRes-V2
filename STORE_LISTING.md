## Screenshots (1280x800 or 640x400)

You need 1-5 screenshots. Here's what to capture:

### Screenshot 1: Google Images with HiRes badges
- Go to Google Images and search for something visual (e.g., "landscape photography")
- Show the grid with resolution badges visible on images
- Capture at 1280x800

### Screenshot 2: High-res image revealed
- Click on an image in Google Images
- Show the full-resolution image loaded in the side panel
- Capture the browser showing the crisp, high-res result

### Screenshot 3: Right-click context menu
- Right-click on any image on a webpage
- Show the "Upscale with HiRes" context menu option
- Capture at 1280x800

### Screenshot 4: Upscale Studio - Before
- Open Upscale Studio with a source image loaded
- Show the clean interface with the source panel visible
- Capture at 1280x800

### Screenshot 5: Upscale Studio - After
- Show a processed/upscaled image result
- Include the download button visible
- Capture at 1280x800

**Tips:**
- Use a clean browser window (no other tabs/bookmarks visible)
- Hide your browser's bookmark bar for cleaner shots
- Use high-quality sample images for demonstrations
